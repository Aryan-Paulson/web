Cookie Policy

Effective Date: 17 January, 2025

At The Collector's Den, we use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze website traffic, and personalize content and advertisements. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage your preferences.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognize your device and store information about your preferences or past actions to improve your browsing experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly, such as remembering items in your shopping cart and managing user sessions.
  • Performance and Analytics Cookies: Collect information about how you use our website, such as pages visited and time spent on the site. This helps us improve performance and user experience.
  • Functional Cookies: Enable enhanced features, such as remembering your language preferences or region.
  • Advertising and Marketing Cookies: Track browsing activity to deliver tailored advertisements and promotions based on your interests.
  • Third-Party Cookies: Used by third-party services, such as Google Analytics and Facebook Pixel, to improve services and deliver relevant content.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Provide essential website functionality.
  • Analyze website performance and improve user experience.
  • Remember your preferences and customize content.
  • Deliver personalized advertisements and promotions.
  • Understand user behavior for marketing and analytics purposes.

4. Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences in the following ways:

  • Browser Settings: Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can block or delete cookies, but some features may be impacted.
  • Cookie Management Tools: We may provide a cookie consent banner or management tool when you first visit our website to customize your preferences.
  • Opt-Out for Third-Party Cookies: You can opt out of third-party cookies for targeted advertising by visiting platforms like the Network Advertising Initiative (NAI) or Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA).

5. Third-Party Cookies and Services

We work with third-party partners who may use cookies or similar technologies to deliver ads, analyze traffic, or provide other services. Examples include:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics and traffic monitoring.
  • Facebook Pixel: For personalized advertising and audience measurement.

These third parties are responsible for their own cookie practices, and we recommend reviewing their privacy and cookie policies for more information.

6. Data Collected Through Cookies

Cookies may collect the following types of data:

  • IP address and device information.
  • Browser type and version.
  • Pages visited, time spent on the site, and navigation patterns.
  • Location data (if enabled).

All collected data is processed in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

7. Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page, and the "Effective Date" will be revised. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.
